Fatplants prototype release, 2019-02
  • Fatplants is a web service with analysis and visualization tools for plant fat-related genes, proteins and metabolism. It empowers researchers to develop and test hypotheses, and design crops with value-added compositions. In particular, collating data in Fatplants on oilseed engineering through can accelerate the process of knowledge discovery and crop design for biology researchers with little computer skills.
